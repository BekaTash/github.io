Featured Case Study

Financial Services Risk Dashboard

Challenge:

A major financial institution was struggling with fragmented data across multiple systems, making it difficult to monitor compliance with BCBS239 requirements. Monthly reporting was manual, error-prone, and consumed over 40 hours of analyst time per cycle.

Solution:

We developed a comprehensive Risk and Compliance Dashboard that:

  • Integrated data from 5 separate systems
  • Automated daily data refreshes
  • Created visual risk indicators with threshold alerts
  • Implemented drill-down capabilities for detailed analysis
  • Included automated export functionality for regulatory reporting

Results:

  • Reduced monthly reporting time from 40+ hours to under 3 hours
  • Improved data accuracy by eliminating manual compilation errors
  • Enhanced risk visibility with daily instead of monthly updates
  • Enabled proactive compliance management through automated alerts
  • Received commendation from regulatory auditors for transparency and completeness
