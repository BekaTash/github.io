Featured Project

Financial Services Risk Dashboard

Challenge:

A major financial institution was struggling with fragmented data across multiple systems, making it difficult to monitor compliance with regulatory requirements. Monthly reporting was manual, error-prone, and consumed over 40 hours of analyst time per cycle.

Solution:

We developed a comprehensive Risk and Compliance Dashboard that integrated data from 5 separate systems, automated daily data refreshes, created visual risk indicators with threshold alerts, and implemented drill-down capabilities for detailed analysis.

Results:

  • Reduced monthly reporting time from 40+ hours to under 3 hours
  • Improved data accuracy by eliminating manual compilation errors
  • Enhanced risk visibility with daily instead of monthly updates
  • Enabled proactive compliance management through automated alerts
