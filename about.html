About DARC Consulting

Data Analytics and Risk Consulting Excellence

Transforming Data into Strategic Business Value

DARC Consulting was founded with a clear mission: to help organizations transform complex data into actionable insights that drive strategic decisions. With extensive experience in data governance, analytics, business intelligence, and process automation, we specialize in delivering solutions that optimize performance, ensure compliance, and enable data-driven decision-making.

Our expertise spans multiple industries, with particular depth in financial services, telecommunications, and regulated environments where data quality, governance, and compliance are critical concerns.

Meet the Founder

With over 8 years of experience in data governance, analytics, and business intelligence, our founder has held senior roles at leading organizations including Bank of New Zealand, Xero, and NBS.

As a Senior Data Risk & Governance Manager at ANZ bank, they developed expertise in non-financial risk frameworks, risk monitoring, and regulatory compliance including CPG235, BCBS239, and privacy regulations.

Their technical skills include Power BI, SQL, Python, and various data governance frameworks, combined with a deep understanding of how to translate technical capabilities into business value.

This unique combination of technical expertise and business acumen allows DARC Consulting to bridge the gap between complex data challenges and strategic business objectives.
