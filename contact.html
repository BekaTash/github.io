Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit my inquiry?

After submitting your inquiry, you'll receive an automatic confirmation email. Our team will review your message and respond within 24 hours (during business days) to schedule an initial consultation or provide the information you requested.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No, we offer a complimentary 30-minute initial consultation to understand your needs and determine how we can best assist you. During this call, we'll discuss your challenges, objectives, and potential approaches.

Do you work with clients outside of New Zealand?

Yes, we work with clients globally. While our office is based in Auckland, we provide remote consulting services to organizations worldwide through video conferencing, collaborative tools, and when necessary, on-site visits.

How quickly can you start a new project?

Our typical lead time is 2-4 weeks, depending on project scope and current commitments. For urgent needs, we may be able to accommodate faster start times. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial discussions.
